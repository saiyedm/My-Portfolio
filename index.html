ClickShield

ClickShield is a Google Chrome extension designed to help users identify phishing, fraud, or scam websites in real time. This extension leverages an AI-powered backend hosted via Flask, which provides personalized results on whether a website is safe or not. The core functionality is powered by Google Safe Browsing API, ensuring reliable data for web safety checks. Received shoutout from TD Bank for the impact on protection against fraud.

Review Project

Food Bridge

Developed FoodBridge, a real-time platform that connects surplus food from businesses and individuals to local food banks and charities. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase, enabling real-time updates, geolocation-based mapping, and donation tracking. Integrated food safety guidelines to ensure compliance. Currently exploring AI-driven surplus prediction models to optimize donation matching and minimize food waste.
